Project Overview

Scalable Brain is an institutional-grade quantitative trading pipeline designed with military-grade risk management, AI-driven decision making, and complete transparency. Discover the five-layer architecture that powers institutional-grade algorithmic trading.

The Scalable Brain Framework

Scalable Brain is built on the principle that no strategy touches live data until it proves a mathematical edge. The system processes raw market data through five interconnected layers, each designed to add rigor, contextual awareness, and risk management before any trade is executed.

This architecture represents the synthesis of quantitative finance, machine learning, and institutional risk management practices.

Layer 0: Qualification Engine

The foundation. Every trading strategy must pass a rigorous backtest before graduating to live trading.

0

Strategy Qualification

The Mathematical Gatekeeper
Complete

Institutional backtesting framework with rigorous statistical validation. All strategies must meet mathematical criteria before any live deployment.

  • Minimum Requirement: At least 20 trades in historical data
  • Statistical Criteria: Positive Expectancy (average win > average loss)
  • Risk-Reward Validation: Profit Factor > 1.15 (for every $1 risked, make $1.15)
  • Promotion Criteria: Only PROMOTED strategies proceed to Layer 1
  • Rejected Strategies: Marked REJECTED, inserted into Dim_Strategy_Registry
  • Core Philosophy: No strategy touches live data until it proves a mathematical edge

Layer 1: Market Regime Detection

Dynamically classifies market conditions to adapt strategy selection in real-time.

1

Market Regime Detection

The Weather Report
Complete

Dynamically classifies the current market state for each asset independently using unsupervised learning.

  • Algorithm: K-Means clustering on ATR (volatility) + ADX (trend strength)
  • Market Regimes: Trending_HighVol, Trending_LowVol, Ranging_HighVol, Ranging_LowVol
  • Validation: Silhouette Score for cluster quality evaluation
  • Persistence: Regime tags persisted to Fact_Market_Regime_V2 SQL table
  • Adaptability: Regimes update hourly as market conditions change

Layer 2: Live Strategy Bank

Promoted strategies run continuously on incoming market data, generating raw trade signals.

2

Live Strategy Bank

Signal Generators
Complete

Promoted strategies run continuously on incoming H1 candle data, generating raw trade signals.

  • Process Flow: PROMOTED strategies from Layer 0 scan live price action
  • Output: Raw BUY/SELL signals with ATR-based stop/target distances
  • Signal Routing: Signals are NOT sent to the broker—they go to the AI Gatekeeper (Layer 3)
  • Timeframe: H1 (hourly) candles across all monitored pairs
  • Data Ingestion: Successfully ingested over 915,400 raw mathematical signals across 3 assets into Fact_Signals database

Layer 3: ML Meta-Labeler

The AI Gatekeeper filters raw signals using machine learning contextuality.

3

ML Meta-Labeler

The AI Gatekeeper
Complete

XGBoost classification model that contextualizes and filters raw signals. It does not predict price—it evaluates trade probability in the current regime using a dynamic 1:3 ATR-based target variable.

  • Model Architecture: XGBoost with 300 boosting rounds and depth 4
  • Features: EMA_50, EMA_200, RSI, ADX, ATR, session flags, H4 trend alignment
  • Temporal Context: London/NY session flags, London-NY overlap detection
  • Approval Threshold: Trades must score > 0.75 confidence to be APPROVED
  • Training Data: 70/30 chronological split on historical signals
  • Dynamic Target: Hunts for asymmetrical Risk:Reward paths (Stop Loss = 1 ATR, Take Profit = 3 ATR)
  • ML Bake-offs: We conduct "ML Bake-offs" using Optuna to tune XGBoost, LightGBM, Random Forest, and PyTorch LSTMs

Layer 4: Dynamic Risk & Portfolio Correlation

Trade-level risk management and portfolio-level correlation guards.

4

Dynamic Risk & Portfolio Correlation

The Shield
In Progress

Two-part risk layer: trade-level ATR stops and portfolio-level correlation guard.

  • Trade-Level Risk (Layer 4): Stop Loss = Close − 1.0 ATR, Take Profit = Close + 2.0 ATR for buys
  • Fixed Pip Prohibition: No fixed pip stops—all stops and targets are ATR-derived
  • Portfolio Correlation (Layer 4.5): 30-day rolling correlation matrix blocks correlated trades
  • Example Correlation Guard: Blocks EUR_USD buy if GBP_USD buy already open (USD correlation)
  • Risk-Reward Ratio: Strict 1:2 R/R across all setups

Core Principles

  • Mathematical Rigor: Every decision is backed by quantitative analysis and statistical validation
  • Transparent Risk: All stops, targets, and position sizes are calculated from market volatility (ATR), not arbitrary pip counts
  • Institutional Grade: Multi-layer checks, audit trails, and correlation guards prevent catastrophic losses
  • AI Contextuality: Machine learning models understand regime, session, and correlation context—not blind prediction
  • No Black Box: Every signal, veto, and trade is traceable to its underlying reason
